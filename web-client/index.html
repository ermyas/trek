How does it work?

Each puzzle contains a series of questions,
the answer to each could be anywhere on earth.

1. Choose a puzzle
Puzzles vary in difficulty, harder puzzles require you to be more accurate in your guesses.
2. Pan and zoom
Center the map on the location that you think the question is referring to.
3. Submit guess
If you're guess is right, you'll move be given another clue.
